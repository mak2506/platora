Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 5, 2026

1. Transparency First

Fluduro is a botanical personality quiz designed purely for entertainment and gaming. We believe in complete transparency regarding your data. We do not sell, rent, or trade your information.

2. Data Usage & Storage

We do not store your personal data long-term. All quiz progress and results are stored in temporary, session-only memory to power the game. Once your session ends or is cleared, this data is gone. We do not maintain a permanent database of your identity or answers.

3. ChatGPT Conversation History

Fluduro operates as an MCP server within the ChatGPT environment. It is important to note that we do not have access to your ChatGPT conversation history. We only receive the specific inputs you provide directly to the Fluduro tool during your session.

4. Third-Party Processing

To generate creative plant-themed questions and analyze your botanical personality, we use Groq Cloud LLM services. Your session-based quiz answers are processed by Groq to generate results. These third parties do not use your data for any other purpose than fulfilling the request within the game.

5. Contact Us

For any questions regarding our minimal data practices, please contact us at support@fluduro.com.
